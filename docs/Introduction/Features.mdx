Reedroux has a bunch of features packed into it to make sure **you** have the most amount of fun with your friends as possible.

---

Reedroux provides a great variety of customizability when it comes to your favorite playlists. You can add your own songs to a saved playlist and use it later! Bring your tunes with you with a few easy commands. It's as simple as that.

Check out [this](/playlists) for more information on how to create and manage your own custom playlists.
