After [inviting Reedroux into your server](/adding-reedroux), you can get the party started! To begin playing songs through Reedroux, follow this guide:

## 1. Join a Voice Channel.

- You can do that by clicking on the channel with a speaker icon.

  ![Joining a Voice Channel](/img/connect.gif)

## 2. Summon Reedroux into the Voice Channel

- You can make Reedroux join the Voice Channel by typing `-join`.
  ![Joining a Voice Channel](/img/connect.gif)

## 3. Start your music.

- Use the command `-play` to start your music.


:::caution When playing a playlist:

- Make sure the playlist is either `Unlisted` or `Public`. If it is set to `Private`, Reedroux won't be able to play that specific playlist.
:::
